Travel to Skopje

The main airport is Skopje International Airport. Turkish Airlines offers convenient connections through Istanbul (if coming from the West coast), but other major European hubs have a connection to Skopje. There is a shuttle bus that takes passengers from the airport to the city center (the bus ticket is about $4). You can find the timetable here: https://skp.airports.com.mk/en-EN/passenger-guide/to-from-the-airport/page/bus There are also taxis that can take you to the city center (cost is about $20).
